# Andrej Karpathy

## About
Former Director of AI at Tesla, founding member of OpenAI. Creator of influential AI education content and advocate for LLM-powered personal knowledge management. His "LLM Knowledge Base" workflow — indexing raw documents and using LLMs to compile interconnected Obsidian wikis — is the **direct inspiration** for Sung's AI Knowledge Wiki project.
