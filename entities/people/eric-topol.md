# Eric Topol

## About
Cardiologist, scientist, and author. Director of the Scripps Research Translational Institute. Leading voice on AI in medicine, author of "Deep Medicine." Co-authored the landmark Cell paper "The Generative Era of Medical AI" with Harvard Medical School colleagues.
