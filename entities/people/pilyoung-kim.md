# Pilyoung Kim

## About
Developmental psychologist at University of Denver. Director of the Brain, Artificial Intelligence, and Child Center (BAIC). Research focus: children's responses to AI systems, problematic AI attachment, and the developmental costs of AI companions. Previously studied child brain development and early relationships before pivoting to AI-child interaction.
