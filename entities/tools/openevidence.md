# OpenEvidence

## About
AI-driven clinical decision support platform that synthesizes peer-reviewed medical literature to answer clinical questions in real time. Founded by Daniel Nadler, launched via Mayo Clinic accelerator. Partners with NEJM and JAMA. Scored 90%+ on USMLE. Used daily by 40%+ of US physicians across 10,000+ hospitals. HIPAA compliant. Valued at $3.5B.
