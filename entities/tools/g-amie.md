# g-AMIE (Guardrailed Articulate Medical Intelligence Explorer)

## About
A multi-agent AI system for medical patient intake developed by [[Google DeepMind]]. Consists of a clinical dialogue agent, guardrail agent, and SOAP note generation agent. Designed to collect patient history while strictly refraining from diagnosis, leaving medical decisions to supervising physicians through asynchronous oversight.
