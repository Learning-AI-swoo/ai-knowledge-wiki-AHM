# UNESCO

## About
United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization. Published the comprehensive 165-page report "AI and the future of education: Disruptions, dilemmas and directions" (2025), establishing a framework prioritizing public purpose, teacher sovereignty, equity, and explainability in AI-education integration.
